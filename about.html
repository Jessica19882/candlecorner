How Our Candles Are Made:

​Each of our candles and our wax melts are made by hand.
We use a white beeswax mix for a slow burn!
With this type of wax, you will see little to no smoke!
White beeswax also holds the essential oils in very well so you get the best scent throw possible!
Each candle is customized and designed with various essential (non toxic) oils, real dried flowers and glitter. Flowers and glitter are always optional of course!
The products are made using a double boiler, mixing tools, beakers and thermometers to ensure each candle is poured at and is cooling at the correct temperatures!
Most candles are a 2 day pour to avoid sinking of your candle! We use various molds for the wax melts!
We are always learning and innovating so our candle and wax melt styles are often changing!
Just be assured that we always continue to put the same level of care into all that we do!
Whether candles will be shipped or picked up locally, we find packaging to be of the upmost importance in order for your candle to arrive undamaged!
If you would like to know more or have questions/concerns, feel free to use our chat option or email option to reach us!
